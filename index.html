Hyun-Hwan Jeong

Postdoctoral Associate

I am a postdoctoral associate in Department of Molecular and Human Genetics at the Baylor College of Medicine and Jan and Dan Duncan Neurological Research Institute. I have received my Ph.D. degree in computer science from Ajou University, in August 2015, and started the postdoctoral associate position in the lab of Dr. Zhandong Liu, and co-mentored by Dr. Huda Zoghbi, since September 2015.

During the Ph.D. studies, I was highly focused on the development of integrative network analysis framework for multiple omics data using information-theoretic measure. I am now focusing on developing quantification algorithm and computational pipeline construction for NGS data for the neurological disorder.
